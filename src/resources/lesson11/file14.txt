<h1>Oracle Academy</h1>

<p>
Few subjects will open as many doors for students in the 21st century as computer science (CS). The Oracle Academy provides resources to help awaken and deepen student interest in this important field of study.
</p>
<p>
The <a href="http://academy.oracle.com">Oracle Academy</a> provides a complete portfolio of software, curriculum, hosted technology, faculty training, support, and certification resources to K-12, vocational, and higher education institutions for teaching use. Faculty can flexibly insert these resources into computer science and business programs, ensuring that students gain industry-relevant skills prior to entering the workforce. The Oracle Academy supports over 1.5 million students in 95 countries. Oracle Academy recently expanded its curriculum to include Java.
</p>
<p>
Oracle Academy offers a wide range of resources for educators and students. Resources for educators are available in three program options: Introduction to Computer Science, Advanced Computer Science, and Business Applications. Professionally-developed courseware is a core component of all 3 programs the Oracle Academy offers. Courses are modular and aligned to Oracle certification, regional, national and state standards, ACM standards, and the U.S. Advanced Placement Computer Science A exam. 
</p>
<p>
To learn more, visit <a href="https://academy.oracle.com/oa-web-introcs-curriculum.html">Oracle Academy Java Programming.</a>
